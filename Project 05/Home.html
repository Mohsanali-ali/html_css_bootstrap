OUR MISSION & SERVICES

Kharee Shareef Death Committee is more than just support during funerals we’re a united community. Our mission is to stand beside every grieving family with honor, compassion, and practical help.

We Provide:
  • Funeral and burial coordination
  • Financial aid for funeral expenses
  • Community death announcements
  • Record keeping of members and services
  • Transparent use of contributions
  • Regular meetings and updates
