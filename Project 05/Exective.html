COMPOSITION OF THE EXECUTIVE
COMMITTEE

  • Comprised of 6 to 10 members, including founding and elected members.
  • Founding Members are permanent unless they resign or pass away.
  • Elected Members are chosen annually at the Annual General Meeting (AGM).
  • Members missing 3 consecutive meetings or attending less than 70% of meetings may be removed.
  • Vacancies are filled by the committee itself.
