KSDC FOUNDER SERVICES

We honor the memory and legacy of the founding members of the Kharee Shareef Death Committee. Their commitment, compassion, and service became the backbone of this community-driven initiative, offering support and dignity during times of loss.

1: MUHAMMAD DEEN (LATE), SEEM

A committed community leader known for his tireless service and unwavering leadership. His vision and dedication laid the groundwork for the committee’s long-term success and sustainability.

2: MUHAMMAD AZEEM (LATE), MOHRA SURAT

A respected and selfless individual, Muhammad Azeem played a crucial role in organizing and managing support for families during their most difficult moments. His compassion lives on through the committee’s ongoing work.

3: MUHAMMAD BASHIR (LATE), MOHRA SURAT

A humble yet influential figure, Muhammad Bashir’s dedication and foresight helped establish the guiding principles of the committee. His legacy is reflected in every act of care the committee extends today.

4: KARAMAT HUSSAIN (LATE), DOHALLA

A man of integrity, service, and deep compassion, Karamat Hussain is remembered for his relentless commitment to the well-being of grieving families. His contributions continue to inspire.

SERVICES PROVIDED BY HONORABLE MEMBERS

These esteemed individuals have played — and continue to play — a vital role in supporting the mission of the Kharee Shareef Death Committee. Their ongoing dedication and compassionate service have strengthened the community during times of need.

5: HAJI KABIR HUSSAIN, DHOK BAGOO

A generous and active supporter, Haji Kabir Hussain is known for his consistent involvement in logistical arrangements and financial assistance. He is always at the forefront during funeral preparations and community events, ensuring every process runs smoothly with respect and dignity.

6: HAJI MUHAMMAD YOUSAF, RATTER

As a retired educator, Haji Muhammad Yousaf brings wisdom and structure to the committee’s efforts. He offers guidance, keeps detailed records, and mentors younger volunteers. His disciplined approach and calm demeanor are a pillar of strength for the team.

7: HAJI ALLAH DITTA, DOHALLA

Known for his sincerity and commitment, Haji Allah Ditta actively assists in coordinating funeral services and mobilizing community resources. His reliability and quick response in emergency situations make him a deeply valued member.

8: HAJI KHALID HUSSAIN, MOHRA SURAT

A key figure in community engagement, Haji Khalid Hussain helps manage outreach efforts and ensures that all families, regardless of background, receive timely support. His efforts in creating unity and compassion within the community are truly commendable.
