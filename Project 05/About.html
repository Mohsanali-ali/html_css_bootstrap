ABOUT KHAREE SHAREEF DEATH COMMITTEE

Collectively working together to support our community with dignity and compassion.

Who We Are

The Kharee Shareef Death Committee is a structured, community-led organization dedicated to supporting families during times of loss. Based on a contributory membership model, our mission is to ease the emotional and financial burden associated with funeral arrangements. The committee was established in Hong Kong in 1979 and officially registered in 2025.

Our Mission

Our aim is to reduce the financial burden on family members by providing a collective membership scheme. Together, members contribute annually to ensure timely funeral and burial arrangements whether in Hong Kong or abroad.

Our Plan Includes

  • Annual contributory membership
  • Financial support for funeral arrangements
  • Assistance even if death occurs outside Hong Kong
  • Support for members, their spouses, and children under 18
  • Burial coordination without burdening families
  • Compassionate communication with organizations and families

Our Aims & Objectives

  • To spare relatives from the stress of arranging a funeral during grief
  • To ensure no family faces financial strain during burial
  • To support fathers, mothers, and children under 18
  • To provide complete liaison with concerned bodies
  • To coordinate all logistics and legal formalities related to death

Our Motto

“Collectively work together to eliminate the burden of the Pakistani community in Hong Kong.”
