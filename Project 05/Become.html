COMMITTEE & MEMBERSHIP

The Kharee Shareef Death Committee is a community-driven, non-profit organization based in Hong Kong. We operate under a transparent and democratically elected leadership to ensure support and dignity during times of loss.

Executive Committee

Overview

The Kharee Shareef Death Committee is governed by an Executive Committee comprising 6 to 10 members. The committee includes founding members (permanent) and elected members (renewable annually).

Commitee Responsibilities

Overseeing day-to-day operations

Managing finances and contributions

Making executive decisions

Organizing meetings and community events

Coordinating with external bodies during funeral arrangements

Meetings

Monthly committee meetings

Special meetings upon request

AGM held within the first 6 months of each financial year

Voting & Decisions

1/3 majority quorum required

Simple majority rules

President holds casting vote

Meetings start with prayer, no political/sectarian discussions
